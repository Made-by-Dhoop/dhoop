About the firm

A trusted name in Bournemouth law since 1987.

Harwick & Co has been guiding individuals and families through life's most important legal moments for over three decades. We've helped thousands of clients buy their first home, protect their estate, navigate difficult family situations, and resolve disputes — always with clarity, care, and no unnecessary complexity.

We're regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and carry full professional indemnity insurance. Every client matters to us — whether your matter is large or small.
