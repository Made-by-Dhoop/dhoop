What We Check

A full technical audit — not a surface-level scan

Most free website checkers give you a score and nothing else. A Dhoop health check gives you a full breakdown of what's wrong, why it matters, and what needs to change.

01
Google Lighthouse Audit

We run a full Lighthouse test across Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, and SEO — and explain what every score actually means for your business.

02
Core Web Vitals

LCP, CLS, and FID — the three metrics Google uses as ranking signals. We check your real-world scores and identify exactly what's causing issues.

03
Page Speed & Load Time

We test your load time on both desktop and mobile, identify the heaviest assets, and flag anything that's slowing your site down unnecessarily.

04
On-Page SEO

Meta titles, descriptions, heading structure, canonical tags, image alt text — we check every on-page SEO element and flag anything that's missing or wrong.

05
Mobile Performance

Over half of all web traffic is mobile. We test your site on real mobile conditions — not just a resized browser window — and flag layout or speed issues.

06
Technical Structure

We review your site's HTML structure, use of semantic elements, schema markup, sitemap, and robots.txt to identify anything that could hurt your search visibility.

What Good Looks Like

The metrics we measure you against

These are the performance benchmarks we use when auditing your website — the same standards Google uses to evaluate and rank sites. Most websites fall well short. Here's what we're aiming for.

Lighthouse Performance Score

Overall performance score — anything below 90 is costing you rankings and conversions.

90+
Largest Contentful Paint (LCP)

How fast your main content loads. Google's threshold for "good" is under 2.5 seconds.

<2.5s
Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)

How stable your page is as it loads. A high CLS means elements are jumping around — damaging UX and rankings.

<0.1
First Input Delay (FID)

How quickly your site responds to user interaction. Slow response times signal a poorly built site to Google.

<100ms
Mobile Performance Score

Your site's performance on mobile devices specifically — often significantly worse than desktop.

85+
