About Dhoop

We build
websites
the right way.

Dhoop is a small team of designers and developers based in Bournemouth. We exist because too many businesses are being let down by slow, bloated, poorly-built websites.

No shortcuts. No page builders. No excuses.

Why We Exist

The web is full of websites that don't work properly

Most websites are built for convenience — not performance. Page builders, bloated themes, and cut-and-paste templates have made it easy to put something online. But easy isn't the same as good.

Slow load times, weak SEO signals, poor structure — these aren't minor issues. They cost businesses real money in lost rankings, lost visitors, and lost conversions. Dhoop exists to fix that.

The web deserves
to be built better
How We Work

A small team. A clear process. No compromises.

Dhoop is a small, focused team of designers and developers. There are no account managers between you and the people building your website. No handoffs, no Chinese whispers, no corners cut to hit a budget.

Every project starts with a proper brief. We take time to understand your business, your goals, and what success actually looks like for you — before we design a single screen or write a single line of code.

Design and development happen together. Our designers and developers work side by side, which means performance is considered from the very first decision — not bolted on at the end.

We measure everything against real standards. Every build is tested against Google Web Vitals, Lighthouse, and Core Web Vitals before it goes anywhere near a live server.

And we're honest. If your brief isn't achievable in your budget, we'll tell you. If your existing site can be improved rather than rebuilt, we'll tell you that too. We'd rather give you the right answer than win the job.

What We Believe

The things we won't compromise on

01

Performance is not optional

Speed is not a feature you add at the end — it's a design decision you make at the beginning. Every choice we make, from structure to imagery to code, is made with performance in mind.

02

We don't use page builders

Wix, Squarespace, Elementor, Divi — these tools make building websites easy. They also make them slow, bloated, and hard to optimise. We hand-code everything, because the results are better.

03

Clean code is good design

A well-built website isn't just visually strong — it's structurally sound. Semantic HTML, efficient CSS, and lean JavaScript aren't just good engineering. They're what makes a website fast, accessible, and easy for Google to understand.

04

Honesty over salesmanship

We won't oversell you a solution you don't need. We'll tell you what your website actually needs, what it will cost, and what results you can realistically expect. That's the only way to build a relationship worth having.
