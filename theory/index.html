About — Theory
Scroll for Contact
THEORY was built on the idea that good clothing already exists.

Hidden in the rhythm of London, THEORY is a curated second-hand store shaped by vintage culture, timeless design, and everyday individuality. We source pieces with character — worn leather, faded denim, oversized tailoring, old sportswear, heavyweight basics — clothing that carries history and still feels relevant now.

Every item is chosen for its quality, fit, texture, and story. No fast fashion. No throwaway trends. Just garments made to be worn again, styled differently, and kept longer.

THEORY is for people who value expression over labels and authenticity over excess. A space where vintage meets modern street culture, and where personal style comes before everything else.

London inspired. Vintage driven. Worn with intention.
