About the firm

35 years of getting
it done properly.

Harwick & Co has been a trusted name in Bournemouth law since 1987 — built on word of mouth, repeat clients, and a simple commitment to doing the job right.

The firm

Built on reputation,
not advertising.

Harwick & Co was founded in 1987 by James Harwick with a single principle: give every client the kind of legal advice you'd want for yourself. Clear, honest, and without unnecessary complexity.

Over three decades later, that principle hasn't changed. The firm has grown from a two-person practice to a team of six specialists, covering conveyancing, wills and probate, family law, dispute resolution, and employment matters.

We've never needed to advertise. Every new client has come through a referral — from a neighbour, a friend, a previous client. That's the standard we hold ourselves to.

We're authorised and regulated by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and carry full professional indemnity insurance on every matter we handle.

How we work

The principles we
don't compromise on.

01

Plain English, always

Legal jargon exists to confuse. We don't use it. Every piece of advice we give is explained in language you can understand and act on.

02

Fixed fees where possible

Uncertainty about cost is stressful. Wherever we can, we offer fixed-fee services so you know exactly what you're paying before we start.

03

We return calls

One of the most common complaints about solicitors is that they don't respond. We do. Every message gets a response within one working day.

04

No surprises on the invoice

What we quote is what you pay. We don't add hidden extras or find reasons to expand the scope without your agreement first.
