Legal Services

Everything you need,
handled properly.

From buying your first home to protecting your estate — we cover the legal matters that matter most to individuals and families across Bournemouth and Dorset.

Property Law

Conveyancing

Buying or selling a home is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll make. We handle the entire legal process from start to finish — searches, contracts, exchange, and completion — so you can focus on the move itself.

We also handle remortgages, transfer of equity, leasehold transactions, and new-build purchases. Fixed fees available on standard residential transactions.

What's included
  • Residential purchases and sales
  • Remortgage and equity release
  • Leasehold and new-build transactions
  • Transfer of equity
  • Help to Buy and shared ownership

Estate Planning

Wills & Probate

A professionally drafted will is one of the most important things you can do for the people you care about. We make the process straightforward — asking the right questions and ensuring your wishes are clearly expressed and legally valid.

When someone passes away, we guide families through the probate process with sensitivity and clear advice, handling the legal and administrative complexity so you don't have to.

What's included
  • Will drafting and review
  • Lasting Powers of Attorney
  • Grant of probate
  • Estate administration
  • Inheritance tax advice

Family Law

Family Law

Divorce, separation, and family breakdown are among the most difficult experiences anyone goes through. Our approach is calm, considered, and focused on achieving the best outcome for you and your family — not on making the process more adversarial than it needs to be.

We specialise in reaching agreements wherever possible, using mediation and negotiation before resorting to court proceedings.

What's included
  • Divorce and separation
  • Financial settlements
  • Child arrangements and custody
  • Cohabitation agreements
  • Pre-nuptial agreements
Book a Consultation
Civil Litigation

Dispute Resolution

When disputes arise — whether with a neighbour, a contractor, a landlord, or a business — we help you find the quickest and most cost-effective path to resolution. Going to court is rarely the right first step, and we always explore alternatives first.

Where litigation is necessary, we represent clients clearly and effectively throughout the process.

What's included
  • Neighbour and boundary disputes
  • Contract disagreements
  • Landlord and tenant matters
  • Debt recovery
  • Mediation and negotiation
